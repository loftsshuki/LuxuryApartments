1. Campaign Concept & Strategy

1.1 The Core Concept

The Digital Nomad World Tour

Select 5 diverse micro-influencers (25-35 years old) from tech and finance backgrounds who embody the modern remote worker lifestyle. Provide them with free luxury accommodations for one month in 12 different cities over the course of a year, creating the ultimate "work from anywhere" dream scenario.

Their Mission: Live, work, and showcase the luxury apartment lifestyle while creating authentic content about their experiences, the properties, and the cities they explore.
