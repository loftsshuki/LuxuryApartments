Valuation Methodology

These valuations are based on analysis of recent domain sales, industry trends, and the specific characteristics of each domain. Key factors considered include:

Market Comparables: Recent sales of similar real estate domains provide the strongest indicator of value. For example, the sale of Homes.com for $35 million in 2021 set a new benchmark for premium real estate domains.

Commercial Potential: Domains that directly match high-value search terms and business categories command premium prices due to their lead generation potential.

Brandability: Short, memorable, and pronounceable domains have higher value. Single-word .com domains are particularly valuable.

Industry Trends: The real estate sector continues to shift online, increasing the value of premium category-defining domains.

Actual sale prices may vary based on negotiation, payment terms, buyer motivation, and current market conditions.

Note: Domain valuations fluctuate based on market conditions. These estimates are based on 2023 market data and comparable sales. A professional appraisal is recommended for precise valuation.
